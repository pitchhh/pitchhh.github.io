Best Hong Kong Hikes for Lazy Hikers

This weekend’s weather is finally looking good. You want to go hiking but you don’t want to spend more than 2 hours nor step out of Hong Kong Island. You also don’t want to visit the Peak or Dragon’s Back for the nth time. Here’s my top 5 alternative hikes with amazing views that can be completed within a couple hours, just in time for your free flow brunch!

1. Red Incense Burner Summit

Time taken: 40 min one-way from Tin Hau MTR

2. Brick Hill

Time taken: 30 min one-way

3. Quarry Bay to Mount Parker

Time taken: 1.5 hours from Quarry Bay MTR

4. Cape Collinson

Time taken: 30 min from Siu Sai Wan Promenade

5. Violet Hills to Repulse Bay

Time taken: 1 hour
